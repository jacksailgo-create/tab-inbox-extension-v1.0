# Code of Conduct

Tab Inbox follows the Contributor Covenant Code of Conduct, version 2.1.

## Our pledge

We pledge to make participation in this project a harassment-free experience for everyone, regardless of age, body size, disability, ethnicity, sex characteristics, gender identity and expression, level of experience, education, socioeconomic status, nationality, personal appearance, race, caste, color, religion, or sexual identity and orientation.

## Expected behavior

- Be respectful and constructive.
- Assume good intent, but take responsibility for impact.
- Keep discussions focused on the project and its users.
- Give and receive technical feedback with care.

## Unacceptable behavior

- Harassment, intimidation, or discrimination.
- Personal attacks or sustained disruption.
- Publishing private information without permission.
- Sexualized language or unwanted attention.

## Enforcement

Project maintainers may remove comments, close issues, block participants, or take other reasonable action when this code is violated.

Instances of abusive behavior may be reported to the maintainers. Reports will be reviewed and addressed as promptly and fairly as possible.
